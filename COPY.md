# La Tribu — Copy & Tone of Voice

## Brand Voice

**Warm, inclusive, high-energy.** La Tribu speaks like a friend inviting you to the best night out. The tone is confident but never exclusive — everyone is welcome to join the tribe.

### Principles

1. **Community first** — "we" not "I", "our nights" not "the events"
2. **Bilingual flavor** — Sprinkle Spanish naturally (La Tribu, fiesta, familia, ritmo) but keep primary copy in English
3. **Action-oriented** — Every section drives toward a CTA
4. **Authenticity** — No corporate speak. Real energy, real people, real culture

### Do

- Use short, punchy sentences
- Address the reader directly ("you", "your")
- Lead with emotion, follow with detail
- Mix English and Spanish naturally

### Don't

- Use jargon or marketing buzzwords
- Over-explain — let the visuals and energy speak
- Be formal or stiff
- Use "club night" — say "event", "night", "fiesta"

## Page Copy Guidelines

### Landing (index.html)

- **Hero**: One bold statement + one-line subtitle. E.g. "London's Hottest Latin Nights" / "Where the city finds its ritmo"
- **Events section**: 4 upcoming event cards with date, venue, vibe description
- **Community section**: Why La Tribu exists — belonging, culture, dance
- **CTA**: "Get Your Tickets" (→ Fatsoma), "Join the Tribe" (→ email signup)

### DJs (djs.html)

- Short bio per DJ (2-3 sentences max)
- Genre tags (salsa, reggaeton, bachata, etc.)
- Tone: hype them up, make them sound unmissable

### Promotions (promotions.html)

- Clear deal descriptions: what you get, conditions, deadline
- Urgency language: "This week only", "Limited spots"
- Partner offers: brief partner intro + the deal

### Past Events (past-events.html)

- Event recap: 2-3 sentences capturing the vibe
- Photos section (authenticated): "Sign in to relive the night"
- Nostalgic but forward-looking: "You were there. Next one's even bigger."

### Venues (venues.html)

- **B2B tone shift**: still warm, but more professional
- Value prop: "We bring the crowd. You keep bar & food revenue. We keep the door."
- Social proof: past venue partnerships, attendance numbers
- CTA: "Let's Talk" (→ contact/email)

## CTA Language

| Action              | Copy                     | Destination     |
|---------------------|--------------------------|-----------------|
| Buy tickets         | "Get Your Tickets"       | Fatsoma link    |
| Email signup        | "Join the Tribe"         | Supabase insert |
| WhatsApp group      | "Join Our WhatsApp"      | QR code / link  |
| View photos         | "Sign In to See Photos"  | Supabase Auth   |
| Venue partnership   | "Let's Talk"             | Email / form    |
| DJ booking          | "Book [DJ Name]"         | Email / form    |
